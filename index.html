Mobile News!

Mobile MMORPGs are becoming progressively more prevalent within the larger MMO genre. A lot of them are still very dated, very limited, but some upcoming Mobile MMOs look promising, and are entirely cross-platform compatible. Much like traditional PC MMORPGs, Mobile MMOs come in several types: Free to Play MMORPGs, Pay to Play MMORPGs, and Buy to Play MMORPGs. Every type of MMO, and consequently every type of game varies in quality – but there is no larger discrepancy in terms of quality than the difference between these 3 business models. In this list, you’ll find what is arguably one of the largest, most complete selections of Mobile MMORPGs – each with their own corresponding reviews, images, news and fully dedicated gameplay. Because, as gamers.. we have a lot to say.

Tower of Fantasy

Previous Next

Tower of Fantasy is a free-to-play MMORPG. It utilizes what seems to be very high quality, very fast, very smooth action combat, however we haven’t seen enough of the combat to get a proper gauge.

The game utilizes a large, open world filled with seamless transitions, allowing players large areas to explore, questing in, exploring to your hearts content. There are dungeons, raids, and various PvP modes to engage in.

V4

Previous Next

V4 is a free-to-play MMORPG. It utilizes tab-target combat with the ability to set various different abilities to your ability wheel, but as this is a cross-platform MMO at the same time your abilities are highly limited.

The game utilizes segregated zones, allowing players large areas to explore, questing in, fighting other players and trying to survive. There are dungeons, raids, and a pretty extensive PvP mode (Lunatra) to engage in.
